# Code of Conduct

## Short Version

Be respectful. Don't be a jerk. Help people root their phones.

## Long Version

### Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in RootDB a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

### Our Standards

**Do:**
- Be welcoming and inclusive
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Verify root information before submitting — wrong guides can brick phones

**Don't:**
- Harass, troll, or insult other participants
- Publish others' private information
- Submit intentionally wrong or dangerous root instructions
- Spam submissions to farm trust points
- Use sexualized language or imagery

### Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **conduct@rootdb.xyz** or via GitHub's reporting tools.

Responses may include:

1. **Warning** — Private written warning
2. **Temporary ban** — Temporary loss of submission privileges
3. **Permanent ban** — Permanent removal from the project

### Dangerous Content

Submitting intentionally dangerous root instructions (guides designed to brick devices, fake "unlock" tools containing malware) will result in an **immediate permanent ban**.
